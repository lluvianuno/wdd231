Site Plan

More Than Basketball

Site Name

Site Name:More Than Basketball

This name was selected because it clearly represents the focus of the basketball club focused on young or new players. It is simple, descriptive, and easy to recognize for parents, players, and coaches.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to serve as an information hub for a local youth basketball club. The website will provide practice schedules, team information, basic training resources, and a way for parents and players to contact the club or register for the season.

Scenarios

  • What days and times are basketball practices held?
  • How can my child register to join the basketball club?
  • What is the club's mission and values?

Color Scheme

  • #1F2933 (Dark Blue-Gray) - Used for headings, the header background, footer, and primary text.
  • #F97316 (Orange) - Used for accents such as borders, links, and highlighted elements.
  • #F9FAFB (Light Gray) - Used as the main page background color.

Typography

  • Open Sans - Used for body text and paragraphs for clarity and readability.
  • Montserrat - Used as a fallback and for headings when available.

Wireframes

The wireframes for the website will include a homepage with a welcoming message, a schedule page with practice times and locations, a registration page with a form for parents to sign up their children.. A join the club page. The design will be clean and simple, with easy navigation and clear calls to action for parents and players, and a schedule page, with practice times, location, contact information, and coaches name.
